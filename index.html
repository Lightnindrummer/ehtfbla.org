About FBLA

FBLA is an organization that helps expose students to the business world. High schools all around the world are “chapters” of this organization and participate in conferences, competitions, and more. Being a member of FBLA, you will be able to travel, participate in academic competitions, explore career options, network with students from all over the globe, and be eligible for scholarships and discounts from FBLA’s partners and sponsors.

EHT FBLA

Since it was reactivated in 2005, EHT FBLA has become more successful every year. Last year, in the 2019-2020 school year, we reached a record breaking amount of members. In the summer of 2019, we made history with our first first place Nationals win.

NLC

Tens of thousands of FBLA Members all over the world will gather in Anaheim, California for the 2021 National Leadership Conference from June 29th to July 2nd. During this week, we will be able to travel the city, participate in national, regional, and state meetings, compete, and network with other FBLA members.

State Leadership Conference, New Jersey

Thousands of NJ FBLA members gather at Harrah's in Atlantic City for the annual State Leadership Conference. Over the course of three days in March, members participate in the Opening Session, Workshops, Competitions, Awards and Recognition Session, and other fun events like the Dance. The theme for the 2021 NJSLC is CONQUER THE IMPOSSIBLE.
